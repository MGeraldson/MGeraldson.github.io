h1>Week 4 - Internet Business and Marketing

Introduction

This week focuses on key elements of using the internet as a marketing tool. It begins with an overview of internet marketing before going on to discuss the components of an e-marketing strategy including the e-marketing plan, the distinct characteristics of the e-marketing environment, demand and competitor analysis, objective setting and the e-marketing audit. This is followed by an explanation of the marketing mix using the product, price, place and promotion model. The discussion then moves on to the issue of branding and the methods used to target customers or customer segments.
